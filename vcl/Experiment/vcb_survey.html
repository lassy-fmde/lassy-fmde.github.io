Visual Contract Builder. Evaluation Survey

The purpose of this questionnaire is to obtain your feedback concerning your use of the Visual Contract Builder (VCB) tool used during the experiment VCL vs UML+OCL to support the language VCL. The questions are made for both VCB and Papyrus UML.

Please respond to each question as thoughtfully and honestly as you can. Anything you write down will be treated confidentially.

Thank you.
